# Pet Adoption Process

The Furever Home connects you with rescue pets from shelters within 100 miles of the Bay Area. Here's how adoption typically works:

## Steps to Adopt

1. **Browse Pets**: Look through available dogs and cats on our site. Use filters for size, age, gender, and city.

2. **Visit the Shelter**: Contact the shelter directly to schedule a visit. Meet the pet in person to see if you're a good match.

3. **Fill Out an Application**: Most shelters require an adoption application. This includes information about your home, lifestyle, and experience with pets.

4. **Home Check** (sometimes): Some rescues may do a home visit to ensure a safe environment.

5. **Adoption Fee**: Fees typically range $90-$150 for cats and $110-$120 for dogs. This usually covers spay/neuter, vaccinations, and microchipping.

6. **Take Your Pet Home!**: Bring a carrier for cats or a leash/harness for dogs.

## Tips
- Be patient — the process can take a few days to a week
- Adoption fees are tax-deductible at many 501(c)(3) rescues
- Most shelters have a return policy if it doesn't work out
- "Adopt, don't shop" — rescue pets make wonderful companions
