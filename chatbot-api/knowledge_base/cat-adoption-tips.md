# Cat Adoption Tips

Adopting a cat is a wonderful decision! Here's what to know:

## Before Adoption
- Cat-proof your home: secure loose wires, remove toxic plants, block small spaces
- Get essentials: litter box, food/water bowls, scratching post, carrier, toys
- Choose a quiet room as a "safe space" for the first few days

## First Week
- Let the cat explore at their own pace — don't force interaction
- Keep the cat in one room initially, then gradually open up the house
- Maintain a consistent feeding schedule
- Keep the litter box in a quiet, accessible location

## Health
- Schedule a vet visit within the first week
- Keep up with vaccinations and preventive care
- Indoor cats live longer on average (12-18 years vs 2-5 for outdoor)

## Behavior
- Scratching is natural — provide appropriate outlets (scratching posts)
- Cats need vertical space — consider cat trees or shelves
- Play with your cat daily (15-20 minutes) to prevent boredom and obesity
