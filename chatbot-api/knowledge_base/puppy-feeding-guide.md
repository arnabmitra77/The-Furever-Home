# Puppy Feeding Guide

Puppies have different nutritional needs than adult dogs. Here's a general feeding schedule:

- **6-12 weeks**: Feed 4 times per day with puppy-specific food
- **3-6 months**: Reduce to 3 meals per day
- **6-12 months**: Transition to 2 meals per day
- **12+ months**: Most dogs do well on 2 meals per day

Choose a high-quality puppy food that lists real meat as the first ingredient. Avoid foods with artificial preservatives, colors, or fillers. Always provide fresh water.

For large breed puppies, use large-breed specific puppy food to control growth rate and protect developing joints.

Consult your veterinarian for specific portion sizes based on your puppy's breed, size, and activity level.
