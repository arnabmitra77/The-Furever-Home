Our Vision, Mission & Values

🌟 Vision

A Bay Area where every adoptable pet finds a loving forever home.

🎯 Mission

To increase pet adoption by aggregating Bay Area shelters into one platform, making it simple for families to discover and connect with their perfect pet match.

💜 Values

  • Compassion First — Every animal deserves love
  • Transparency — Honest info about each pet
  • Accessibility — Making adoption easy for everyone
  • Community — Partnering with local shelters
  • Responsibility — Encouraging lifelong commitments

📋 The Adoption Process

Step 1: Browse & Match

Use filters or take the quiz. Save favorites to your wishlist.

Step 2: Visit the Shelter

Visit during open hours and spend time with the animal.

Step 3: Complete Application

Fill out the shelter's adoption application.

Step 4: Home Check

Some shelters may require a brief home visit.

Step 5: Pay Fee & Take Home!

Fees include spay/neuter, vaccinations, and microchipping.

Step 6: Post-Adoption Support

Shelters offer support during the adjustment period.

❓ FAQs

What does the match percentage mean?
Based on your quiz answers and pet characteristics. Higher = more compatible. Always visit in person!
How much does adoption cost?
Dogs: $200–$350, Cats: $75–$150. Includes spay/neuter, vaccines, and microchip.
Can I adopt in an apartment?
Yes! Use size and energy filters to find apartment-friendly pets.
What if it doesn't work out?
All partner shelters have return policies. No judgment.
Do you house animals directly?
No. We aggregate listings from partner shelters.

💡 Tips for New Pet Parents

🏡

Prepare Your Home

Remove toxic plants, secure wires, set up a cozy area.

🩺

First Vet Visit

Schedule within the first week. Bring shelter records.

🍽️

Nutrition

Transition food slowly over 7–10 days.

⏰

Establish Routine

Consistent times for feeding, walks, play, and sleep.

💛

3-3-3 Rule

3 days to decompress, 3 weeks to learn routine, 3 months to feel at home.

📋

Get Licensed

Required in most Bay Area cities. Helps reunite if lost.
